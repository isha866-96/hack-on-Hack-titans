AI Traffic Management

1. Data Collection

AI gathers real-time data from traffic cameras, sensors, and GPS to analyze congestion.

2. Traffic Prediction

Machine learning algorithms forecast traffic patterns based on historical and live data.

3. Smart Traffic Signal Control

AI adjusts signal timings dynamically to improve traffic flow and reduce waiting time.

4. Emergency Vehicle Priority

AI provides priority to ambulances and emergency vehicles by adjusting signals accordingly.

5. AI-Powered Traffic Monitoring

Authorities monitor live traffic data and respond to road incidents in real-time.

6. Automated Traffic Enforcement

AI-powered cameras detect traffic violations like overspeeding and illegal parking.

7. Smart Public Transport Optimization

AI suggests the best routes for buses, metros, and carpools based on traffic conditions.

8. Eco-Friendly Traffic Solutions

AI encourages sustainable transportation methods like cycling, electric buses, and shared rides.

9. Predictive Maintenance for Roads

AI predicts road damages and suggests preventive maintenance to avoid accidents.
